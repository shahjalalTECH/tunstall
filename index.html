Tunstall Gem4 Features

The Tunstall Gem4 is designed with a large, easy-to-press SOS button that can be used to raise an alarm in case of an emergency. This button can be used to summon help from up to 4 pre-set emergency contacts, making it a reliable way to get assistance quickly.

The Tunstall Gem4 uses GPS technology to accurately locate the wearer, making it easy for emergency services to find them quickly in case of an emergency. This feature is particularly useful for people who may be at risk of getting lost or disorientated, such as those with dementia or other cognitive impairments.

The Tunstall Gem4 Loved Ones Monitoring Package comes with a management portal that allows contacts to be configured and device settings to be adjusted remotely. This makes it easy to manage the device and keep it up to date with the latest settings.

The Tunstall Gem4 can be equipped with automatic fall detection that can sense if the wearer has fallen and raise an alarm automatically. This is an important feature for people who may be at risk of falling, such as the elderly.

The Tunstall Gem4 comes with a USB-powered charging dock that makes it easy to keep the device charged and ready for use. This charging dock is easy to use and can be set up quickly.

The Tunstall Gem4 is compatible with the 4G mobile network, ensuring reliable and fast connectivity at all times. This feature ensures that emergency alerts are sent and received quickly and efficiently.

The Tunstall Gem4 is rated IP67 for water resistance, which means it can withstand immersion in water for up to 30 minutes at a depth of 1 meter. This makes it suitable for use in wet environments or during activities such as showering.

The Tunstall Gem4 provides device feedback through voice prompts and vibration, making it easy to know when the device is working and when an alert has been sent. This feature is useful for people who may have difficulty seeing or hearing feedback on the device.
