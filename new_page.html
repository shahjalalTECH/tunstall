Discover the Features of the Tunstall Gem4

Easy-to-use SOS Button

With its large, easy-to-use SOS button, the Tunstall Gem4 lets you raise an alarm instantly during emergencies.

Accurate GPS Location

Using GPS technology, the Gem4 accurately pinpoints your location, aiding our Customer Care Centre or your loved ones in finding you promptly.

Automatic Fall Detection

For added safety, you can equip your Gem4 with automatic fall detection, which can detect falls and trigger an alarm.

Convenient Charging

The Gem4 includes a USB-powered charging dock for hassle-free device charging, ensuring it's always ready when needed.

Fast 4G Connectivity

Stay connected with the reliable 4G mobile network, ensuring swift communication of emergency alerts.

Water-Resistant Design

With an IP67 water-resistant rating, the Gem4 can withstand immersion in water for up to 30 minutes at a 1-meter depth, making it suitable for wet environments and showering.
